SECURITY & PRIVACY

Your data is yours. Period.

We built foliologic with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought.

🔒

AES-256 Encrypted

Your portfolio data is end-to-end encrypted with your password using AES-256-GCM. Decryption happens only in your browser — the server only stores ciphertext.

🇪🇺

EU Data Storage

Your encrypted portfolio is stored on European servers (Supabase EU). Price data and AI features use US-based APIs (FMP, Anthropic), but your portfolio data stays encrypted and in the EU.

🤖

AI Data Isolation

Your portfolio data is only sent to AI when you actively use AI features. It's never shared with third parties and never used to train AI models (per Anthropic's API terms). AI results are not stored server-side.

🚫

No Data Selling. Ever.

We make money from subscriptions, period. No ads, no tracking pixels, no affiliate deals, no selling your financial data to anyone.

🏛️

German Team

Built by a team in Germany. Company registration (UG) in progress. Real people, real accountability, real Impressum.

🔑

Client-Side Encryption

Your encryption key is derived from your password and never leaves your device. We cannot decrypt your portfolio data — only you can, with your password.
